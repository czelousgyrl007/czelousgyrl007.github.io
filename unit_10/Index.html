The RAMONES

The Ramones are the godfathers of punk rock. Four friends from Queens, New York formed a band in 1974 and changed music forever. Members Joey, Dee Dee, Johnny, and Tommy (who eventually turned his drums over to Marky) wowed crowds at New York clubs like CBGBs. Explore some of their greatest hits on Blitzkrieg Bop.
